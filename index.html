About TEAM VEERss RACING

TEAM VEERss RACING is the official BAJA team of VSSUT Burla, representing the university in national-level ATV competitions like eBAJA SAE INDIA and ATVC.

The team consists of passionate undergraduate students from multiple engineering disciplines collaborating in design, fabrication, testing, and manufacturing of electric off-road vehicles.

Current Vehicle

E-IOKE IV

Our electric ATV designed for eBAJA SAE INDIA 2026, engineered with focus on endurance, safety, maneuverability, and sustainability.
